## Common Questions

### Task & Responsibility Questions
- "Who must do what?"
- "What tasks were assigned to [person name]?"
- "Who is responsible for [specific task]?"
- "What are the action items?"
- "Who should follow up on [topic]?"

### Decision & Outcome Questions
- "What decisions were made?"
- "What was decided about [topic]?"
- "What conclusions were reached?"
- "What was agreed upon?"

### Timeline & Deadline Questions
- "What are the deadlines?"
- "When is [task/project] due?"
- "What is the timeline?"
- "When should [action] happen?"

### Details & Context Questions
- "What were the main concerns?"
- "Why was [decision] made?"
- "What issues were raised?"
- "What alternatives were discussed?"

### Next Steps Questions
- "What are the next steps?"
- "What happens next?"
- "What should be done first?"
- "What's the priority?"
